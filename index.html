Welcome to MadLibs Adventure!

Unleash your creativity with our interactive MadLibs game. Fill in the blanks with nouns, verbs, and adjectives to create hilarious stories.

How to Play:

  1. Type in different nouns, verbs, and adjectives in the input fields on the right.
  2. As you type, the story on the left will automatically update.
  3. Watch your customized story come to life!

About us

We are a team of five who created this web app as an assignment during our bootcamp DZ-FEW at Recoded. We used HTML, CSS, and JS to create a variant of a Mad Libs game with a story of our choice. This project showcases our collaborative skills and our ability to work with front-end technologies.
